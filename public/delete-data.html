NibaChat Data Deletion Instructions

Last updated: June 27, 2026

If you contacted a business that uses NibaChat and want your conversation or order data deleted, please follow the steps below.

How To Request Deletion

Email njakovljevic21@gmail.com with the subject line Data deletion request.

Please include the business profile you contacted, your Instagram or Facebook username if available, and the approximate date of your message. Do not send sensitive payment information.

What We Delete

We will delete or anonymize conversation records, raw webhook events, profile identifiers, and related support/order notes where deletion is legally and technically possible.

Timing

Deletion requests are usually processed within 30 days after we can verify the request.
