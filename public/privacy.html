NibaChat Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 27, 2026

NibaChat provides customer support automation for Facebook Messenger and Instagram Direct messages for connected business profiles.

Information We Process

When a customer sends a message to a connected business profile, we may process the message text, attached images, sender platform ID, conversation metadata, order details voluntarily provided by the customer, and support notes required to answer the request.

How We Use Information

We use this information only to answer customer questions, identify products, create order or complaint records, route conversations to the business, and improve the business-specific knowledge base.

Sharing

We do not sell personal information. Data is shared only with the connected business owner, configured service providers such as hosting/database providers, Meta APIs, Google Sheets if enabled by the business, and legal authorities when required by law.

Retention

Conversation data is retained for the period configured by the business, typically 30 days, unless a longer retention period is required for order, complaint, security, or legal reasons.

Security

API keys and access tokens are stored encrypted. Access to administrative areas is restricted by login credentials.

Your Choices

You may request access, correction, or deletion of your data by contacting the business you messaged or by using our data deletion instructions.
